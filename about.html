Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven Coffee began as a small roastery in Portland, Oregon. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, wanted to create exceptional coffee that brings people together.

Our Philosophy

We source only the finest beans from sustainable farms and roast them to perfection in small batches. Every cup tells a story of quality and care.
